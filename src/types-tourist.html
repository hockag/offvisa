Tourist Visa

Under certain conditions, you will even be exempted from a visa

What is a tourist visa?

  • A tourist visa is a travel credential that permits individuals to journey to a foreign nation for purposes of leisure or business, such as attending meetings or conferences without any financial compensation. If your intentions involve working, studying, or establishing a business, it is obligatory to seek the appropriate visa category.

  • Distinct regulations and visa categories apply in different countries. In certain scenarios, you might even be exempt from needing a visa. Some countries enable online registration (e.g., ESTA for the USA, ETA for Canada), or they allow you to travel using only your identification (as seen in the Schengen area).

  • Regarding the array of tourist visa types, each country has its own specifications. While some merely allow a brief visit, others provide the flexibility of multiple entries spanning over a substantial period.

  • At times, comparing distinct visa categories and understanding the prerequisites for various countries can be complex. OffVisa aims to facilitate your decision-making process by aiding you in selecting the most suitable option and simplifying the application process.

  • Securing a tourist visa used to involve a trip to the nearest embassy, which incurred both time and financial costs. In recent times, several governments have introduced the eVisa or Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) mechanism, enabling the entire application and approval process to be conducted online. This modernization saves time and mitigates bureaucratic complications.
